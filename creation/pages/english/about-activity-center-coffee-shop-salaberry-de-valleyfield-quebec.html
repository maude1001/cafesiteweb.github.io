About Us

Activity Center / Coffee Shop in Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, Quebec offering Childrens Summer Camps, Family Activities, Ceramic Novelties, Painting Workshops, Corporate Events, and more

Our mission is to create a unique moment in an authentic and warm atmosphere. Each person who walks through the door can get away from their daily lives, spend a pleasant time with family or friends and put a touch of color in their lives. A place where time stands still for a moment to give way to their creativity, a process by which imagination becomes reality.

Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, Montreal, Laval, Vaudreuil-Dorion, Dorval, Kirkland, Pointe-Claire, Châteauguay, Huntingdon, Saint-Antoine-Abbé, Cornwall, Lancaster, Quebec, Alexandria, Hawkesbury, Ontario, and the surrounding areas.
